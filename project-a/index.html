Mycosmic Kaleidoscope

  • Click and hold on any of the colored squares to create patterns.
  • Press any keys from 1 to 0 to change the color palette.
  • Press the “SPACE” bar to clear the page and have a white background.
  • Press “b” on the keyboard to clear the page and have a black background.
  • Press “q” to erase on a white background / “w” to erase on a black background.
  • Press “a” to draw random-sized colorful circles / “s” to draw random-sized colorful circles.
    • Press “ENTER” to save your generated artwork!
